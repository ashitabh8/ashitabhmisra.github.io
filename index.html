About me

Graduating in Summer 2025 and seeking positions for Quant Researcher/Data Scientist/ML Engineer!

I am Akshay Pandit, a 5th year PhD student at the University of Illinois Urbana-Champaign in the Department of Civil and Environmental Engineering.

I am fortunate to be advised by Dr. Megan Konar.

My research is based on finding a causal relationship between Agricultural variables and Trade openness at the global scale and at the bilateral scale for the US-China trade system. For my dissertation, I am working on the following four objectives:

  • Analyze the relationship between global trade and agricultural productivity.
  • Determine the causal impact of global trade on agricultural productivity.
  • Estimate the spatially resolved bilateral trade links in the US-China agri-food system.
  • Ascertain the causal impact of China joining the WTO on US agriculture.
